About Me

Aaron J. Torres is an ambitious and versatile professional hailing from New York, born in Brooklyn and raised on Long Island, currently residing in the vibrant Lower Manhattan. With a high school diploma complemented by art awards and graphic design coursework at Kingsborough Community College, Aaron has always been drawn to creative pursuits, excelling in graphic design and photography—both digital and film formats. His innate curiosity drives him to dissect and enhance the systems around him, whether through optimizing workflows or innovating solutions to everyday challenges. This enthusiasm for personal growth and skill acquisition has been a constant thread throughout his career, fueling his commitment to delivering exceptional results in every endeavor.

Professionally, Aaron began his journey as a creative director at a clothing brand, where he led impactful media campaigns, product editorials, and collaborations between brands, blending his artistic talents with strategic vision to elevate brand presence and engagement. Building on this foundation, he transitioned to an account executive role at a handyman company, leveraging his sales acumen to secure more than 250 referrals, demonstrating his ability to build strong client relationships and drive business success. His hands-on expertise in carpentry and handyman skills, honed through managing service teams, has equipped him with practical problem-solving abilities that extend beyond traditional office settings. Currently, as a compliance officer at a moving company with over four years of experience, Aaron ensures regulatory adherence and operational integrity in a fast-paced environment. These experiences underscore his versatility, blending compliance acumen with creative innovation.

Beyond his professional achievements, Aaron is a beam of energy dedicated to improving the spaces and processes around him. He thrives on learning how systems function and identifying opportunities for efficiency gains, a mindset that aligns seamlessly with his interest in automotive service. Whether wrenching on cars, immersing himself in business books, or maintaining physical fitness, Aaron approaches life with unwavering enthusiasm and professionalism. His diverse skill set—from creative media production to hands-on technical proficiency—positions him as a valuable asset in dynamic industries, where he continues to seek roles that allow him to grow, innovate, and contribute meaningfully. Aaron's formal yet passionate approach ensures he not only meets expectations but exceeds them, always looking forward to the next challenge with optimism and determination.
